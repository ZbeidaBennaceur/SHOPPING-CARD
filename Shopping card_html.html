Typically applied after cleansing and before moisturizing, serums are known for their ability to penetrate deeper layers of the skin, delivering potent ingredients such as vitamins, antioxidants, and hydrating agents.
They can help address issues like fine lines, wrinkles, uneven skin tone, and dehydration, leaving the skin looking smoother, brighter, and more youthful.
